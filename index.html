Welcome! Let's talk cheeseburgers

Over the past 30 years, I have created a fine palette in the exquisite world of Cheeseburgers. I have been all over the world during my travels, and I have always made it a point to stop in and find out what the local cheeseburger tastes like and what twist they have added to it. Based on my findings around the 5 continents I have been on, I have broadened my horizons and have an even better understanding of what a cheeseburger really is and I am here to share what I have learned!

What are some of my favorite things about Cheeseburgers you ask? I'll give you a few reasons as to why...

  • They are DELICIOUS!
  • There is a specific style fo cheeseburger for every event
  • You can eat them everywhere
  • And they have all of the most important food groups for a healthy balanced diet!
